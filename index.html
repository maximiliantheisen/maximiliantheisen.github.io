About Me

I am Maximilian Theisen, a psychologist and experimental philosopher from Germany. I am interested in how philosophical concepts translate to psychological hypotheses. My current research interests include:

  • Moral Uncertainty, Moral Ignorance, Moral Advice
  • Responsibility
  • Folk Metaethics
  • Sense of Agency in the Context of Smart Mobility Devices
